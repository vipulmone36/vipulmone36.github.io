Ninja Slushi MAX

I worked on the Ninja SLUSHi™ during my co-op as a Master's student at Texas A&M University. During its development, the team recognised that the average consumer would find it difficult to know what recipes can be slushed and what recipes would not. We incorporated some error codes to inform the user using some basic physics principles, but I realised more could be done here!

The successor to Ninja SLUSHi™, the Ninja SLUSHi™ MAX brought slush to your home faster and smarter. I drove the development of this smart sub-system - from prototyping with arduino boards to working with cross-functional teams - to be able to deliver on the idea of providing the simplest control to make slush with any set of ingredients!
